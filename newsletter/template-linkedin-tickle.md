# LinkedIn — “tickle” post (daily, with news cycle)

**Goal:** Stop the scroll, state the stake, prove rigor in **one screen**, route to **Jackye** and **WDIWF**. No thread required unless the story demands it.

**Length target:** 900–1,300 characters (roughly 5–8 short lines + CTA).

**Rules**

- First line = **plain-spoken headline** (not clickbait).  
- One sentence on **who is affected**.  
- **Source line:** name **2–4 outlets** + bias labels in parentheses, e.g. `WSJ (Lean right) · NYT (Lean left) · Reuters (Center)`.  
- If you have fewer than four outlets: start with **DEVELOPING — we’re watching X sources:** …  
- End with **one CTA**: link to the **WDIWF** article or day hub (use `utm_source=linkedin`).  
- Sign-off can be **— Jackye** or **— Jackye Clayton | Who Do I Work For**.
