# Enforcement layer — pre-flight and final check

**Purpose:** Make voice, message, and visual choices **non-optional** before anything ships.  
**Precedence:** **MESSAGE > VOICE > DESIGN** (see `docs/MESSAGE_SYSTEM.md`).

---

## Before generating (briefing)

- [ ] **Message:** Truth, why it matters, what people are missing — written in plain language (not yet in Canva / Midjourney).  
- [ ] **Weak test:** Could this headline apply to any competitor or any week? If yes, rewrite.  
- [ ] **Facts:** Charter sourcing rules (`WDIWF_NEWS_CHARTER.md`) — verified vs DEVELOPING, no invented stats.  
- [ ] **Voice skim:** `jackye-voice.md` — no corporate / generic LinkedIn tells.  
- [ ] **Values & mission:** Tone matches charter (work & labor, receipts, inclusion) — **not** borrowed ideology from visual references.  
- [ ] **Mirror:** Reader’s **situation and vocabulary of work** reflected; passes “sharp friend” + “not a generic podcast” tests.  
- [ ] **Inclusion:** Worker classes / impacts named where relevant; no afterthought DEI paragraph.  
- [ ] **Customize or declare:** Used real signals where available; if not, **no fake “you”** — honest general mirror instead.

---

## Visual / poster path only

- [ ] **Spec:** `docs/VIRAL_POSTER_IMAGE_SPEC.md` + `docs/visual-references/README.md` reviewed.  
- [ ] **Borrow:** Composition, hierarchy, tension, emotional effect — **not** ideology, harmful tropes, or real logos.  
- [ ] **Safety:** No glamorized harm; no trademark strip unless cleared; inclusive casting per spec.  
- [ ] **Poster copy mirrors stakes** — headline speaks to **workers’ reality** (mission-aligned), not clever emptiness.

---

## Before finalizing (output review)

- [ ] **Message** still obvious after skimming for 3 seconds (social) or 10 seconds (poster).  
- [ ] **Voice** passes read-aloud; cut filler.  
- [ ] **Design** supports message; if the visual overpowers or obscures the truth, simplify.  
- [ ] **Conflict resolution:** If voice and design fight, **voice wins**. If message and voice fight, **message wins**.

---

## Product / legal

- [ ] **BUILD_BIBLE** verification table: no promises for unshipped features.  
- [ ] **Links / CTAs** match `CHANNEL_PLAYBOOK.md` (WDIWF + Jackye routing).

---

## When to stop and ask

Missing sources, ambiguous ask, or tension with inclusivity / charter → **flag early**; do not ship vague work to fill space.
